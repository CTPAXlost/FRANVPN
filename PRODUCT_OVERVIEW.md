## Executive Summary

**FRANVPN** is a premium, modern VPN client application designed for both mainstream users and power users. It represents a fully independent product built with V2Ray core technology but with custom branding, architecture, and user experience that creates a distinct market identity.

Unlike VPN clients that feel technical or security-focused in an opaque way, FRANVPN combines professional SaaS aesthetics with advanced networking capabilities. The product is secure by default while remaining accessible to everyday users.

## Vision Statement

> "FRANVPN is the VPN client for users who demand both security and quality. Modern. Reliable. Transparent."

### Core Brand Positioning

- **Target Users:** Power users, privacy-conscious professionals, international travelers, content creators, developers
- **Market Position:** Premium consumer VPN (not enterprise)
- **Brand Tone:** Confident, professional, modern, global
- **Design Philosophy:** "Complexity hidden, power visible"

## Product Tiers (Future Monetization)

### FRANVPN Free
- Limited servers (3-5 regions)
- Basic protocols (Shadowsocks, SOCKS5)
- Speed limit (50 Mbps)
- Community support

### FRANVPN Pro
- All servers globally
- All protocols including advanced (VLESS, Trojan, gRPC, QUIC)
- Unlimited speed
- Priority support
- Advanced features (Kill Switch, Split Tunneling, custom DNS)

### FRANVPN Business
- Custom profiles and team management
- Audit logs
- Priority 24/7 support
- Custom branding for enterprises

## Core Technology Stack

- **Backend Core:** V2Ray (modified, not visible to user)

- **Mobile:** Flutter or React Native (iOS & Android)
- **UI Framework:** Modern web-based UI (React/Vue)
- **State Management:** Redux/Pinia
- **Styling:** TailwindCSS + custom component library

Current VPN clients are either:

FRANVPN bridges the gap with a **professional-grade, beautiful, feature-rich client** that anyone can use.
